What is Robbery?

Robbery is stealing money or things from a person by using force or threats. The victim is present at the time of the crime.

United Kingdom 🇬🇧

UK Law on Robbery

  • In the UK, robbery is a serious crime
  • Covered under the Theft Act 1968
  • A person can go to prison for many years
  • Up to life imprisonment in the most serious cases
  • Victim safety is always the top priority
